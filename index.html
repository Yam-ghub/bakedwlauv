Our Story

BakedwithLauv started as a small home bakery with a passion for creating delicious, high-quality cookies that bring joy to every bite. What began as a hobby quickly turned into a beloved local business as word spread about our irresistible treats.

We use only the finest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, and every batch is made with love and attention to detail. Our mission is simple: to create cookies that make people happy and create sweet memories.
